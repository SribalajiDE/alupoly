FRP Equipments

Our FRP (Fibre Reinforced Plastic) equipment line is engineered for high performance, reliability, and versatility across industries. We design and manufacture:

FRP Vessels & Pressure Tanks: Ideal for water treatment, chemical processing, and storage, our vessels are corrosion-resistant, lightweight, and built for long service life. Each unit is tailored to withstand harsh chemicals and extreme environmental conditions, ensuring minimal maintenance and reduced downtime.

FRP Gratings & Cable Trays: Non-conductive, rustproof, and easy to install, these are perfect for industrial walkways, platforms, and electrical installations, offering superior safety and durability.

FRP Doors, Panels, and Roof Sheets: Lightweight yet robust, these components are designed for high-impact areas, providing excellent weather resistance and insulation.

Custom FRP Solutions: We work closely with clients to develop bespoke FRP equipment for unique industrial requirements, ensuring optimal fit and performance.

Key Advantages:

High strength-to-weight ratio

Excellent chemical and corrosion resistance

Low maintenance and long operational life

Customizable for any application

FRP Tanks

Our FRP tanks are trusted by industries for safe, efficient, and long-lasting storage solutions. We offer:

Aquarium Tanks: Manufactured with food-grade resins, our FRP aquarium tanks provide crystal-clear visibility, leak-proof construction, and resistance to algae and corrosion—ideal for public aquariums, research labs, and hobbyists.

Water Tanks: Designed for potable and process water storage, these tanks are lightweight, hygienic, and easy to transport and install. They perform reliably in fluctuating weather and are available in various sizes and capacities.

Chemical Storage Tanks: Engineered to safely store acids, alkalis, and other aggressive chemicals, our FRP chemical tanks offer 100% corrosion resistance, low permeability, and compliance with industry safety standards.

Septic Tanks: Our FRP septic tanks are leak-proof, durable, and easy to install, making them ideal for residential, commercial, and institutional sanitation needs.

Features:

Sizes range from small (0.5 m³/hr) to large (20 m³/hr) capacities

Resistant to UV, weather, and chemical attack

Easy to clean and maintain

Available with handholes for access and inspection

Hydrogen Barrier Coatings

We offer specialized hydrogen permeation-resistant coatings for critical infrastructure:

Aluminide-Based Barriers: By diffusing aluminum into metal substrates, we create intermetallic aluminide layers that dramatically reduce hydrogen permeation—essential for nuclear, chemical, and hydrogen storage applications.

FRP Composite Barriers: Our proprietary FRP coatings act as robust hydrogen barriers, maintaining stability at elevated temperatures and in aggressive environments.

Applications:

Pipelines, reactors, and storage vessels in hydrogen and chemical industries

Components exposed to high-pressure hydrogen or deuterium environments

Key Features:

High stability and resistance to hydrogen saturation

Long-term protection at elevated temperatures

Customizable for various substrate metals
